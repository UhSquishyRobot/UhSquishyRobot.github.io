Some Suggestions for Travelers

If you're considering going on vacation yourself, I have a few pointers for you!

  1. On transportation days (from one city or hostel to another) bite the bullet and commit your whole day to it. Obsessively check the times of your flights/trains/busses and show up at least a half hour early
  2. Buy a small amount of international data for your smartphone. This can help you out of tight spots if you become lost or miss a bus/train
  3. Don't pressure yourself to do more than you really want to. There's nothing wrong with relaxing at a cafe and reading a book
  4. Give yourself rest days. It's easy to burn yourself out, and it's worth it to take a day to just lay in your hostel bed watching youtube or whatever to recharge your batteries.
  5. BUY A BICYCLE! It's SO much fun to explore new places on 2 wheels, and if you get a good deal from a farmer's market (strange place to buy a bike I know) you'll end up saving money on public transit
